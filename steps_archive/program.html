스텝스의 교육과정

문제 파악

Identifying weaknesses Using our in-house proprietary analytical system, we can quickly guage the student's currently level of understanding.

For example, if a student doesn't know that the sum of internal angles of a triangles is 180°, then there's a very high chance that his understanding of trignometry is very shaky. Conversely, if the student is very proficient with logarithm, then it's implicit that she understands how power works and probably also roots.

기본 보충

After we have identified the underlying cause of instability in current understanding, we will supplement the underlying cause as to make it easier for the student to understand more complex ideas.

반복 훈련

At the same time, we will make sure the student gets plenty of practice with the same problem until he knows how to solve the problem completely confidently on his own.
